Echoes In The Night
Echoes In The Night is a horror story about an evil girl, Molly Shuman who witness her family's death except her father Steve. The story place in Boston, Massachusetts. The murder takes place in 1975, at Molly's family home. Her Aunt Lucy takes her in after her father commits suicide and she becomes part The Sisterhood, a cult of witches. She soon realizes her powers and a special mark that she has on her arm. She begins her journey on revenge to anyone that has harmed her or The Sisterhood
Satan's Maidens (Echoe’s Sequel)
Twin sisters Cindy and Lucy discover they are real witches growing up in Salem, Massachusettes. Their mother Charlotte reveals they come from a long lineage of witches. Cindy uses her magic for good and finds true love. Lucy uses her magic for evil and tries to destroy anything that is pure and good. She decides to join The Sisterhood, a group of satanic women who teach her how to become the daughter of the truly evil one, Satan.
Calixia
Calixia is a strong willed teenager who is training to be a professional ballerina. When she begins experiencing stomach pain, she realizes something is terribly wrong. She undergoes surgery to discover she has cancer and may not be alive for her high school graduation. She is faced with the reality she will have to overcome her struggles of being alive to face her fear of death.
